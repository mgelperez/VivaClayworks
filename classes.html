What to Know Before You Register




Class Overview


Each 90 minute class will be on the wheel. You will be given a demonstration and then go step by step along with the instructor, we try our very best to have you make a successful piece. You have the fun, we’ll do the work and call you when it’s done! It takes about 3 weeks to finish all the firing and glazing, we will let you know when they are complete!

Day of Event


Free parking is available in our back lot, accessible from the west side of the building. Wear comfortable clothes and shoes you don’t mind getting messy—aprons are provided. Please remove any jewelry, tie back long hair, and let your instructor know if your nails extend past your fingertips.

Late Policy


Pottery Night Events start on the hour, if you are 15+ minutes late, we will rebook you for another evening.

Cancellation


If you are cancelling a Pottery Night Event, please do so 7 days in advance for full refund. Within 7 days we will try to rebook you.

Refunds


If you wish to request a refund for (a 6-week course, clay and tools), (a 6-week course and clay) or (a 6-week course) a $50 admin fee will be held back.

Gift Voucher


We can also convert your purchase into a gift voucher which would be valid for two years. This would allow you to apply it to a future course, clay, tools, a pottery night experience or purchasing pieces from our shop.
